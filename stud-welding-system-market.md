<p><strong>The Stud Welding System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drawn Arc Stud Welding</li><li>Capacitor Discharge Stud Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stud welding system market comprises two primary types: drawn arc stud welding and capacitor discharge stud welding. Drawn arc stud welding involves heating the stud and base material with an electric arc, allowing for strong welds suitable for thick materials. In contrast, capacitor discharge stud welding uses a stored electric charge to create a quick, high-energy weld, ideal for thin materials and sensitive applications. Both methods are widely used in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries for their efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
