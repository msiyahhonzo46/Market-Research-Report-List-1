<p><ul><li>Isolated Flyback Converter</li><li>Non-isolated Flyback Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flyback converter market is divided into two main types: isolated and non-isolated flyback converters. Isolated flyback converters feature electrical isolation between the input and output, enhancing safety and reliability, making them ideal for applications like power supplies in telecommunications and medical devices. Non-isolated flyback converters, on the other hand, lack this isolation but are simpler and more cost-effective for low-power applications, such as consumer electronics. Both types are vital for efficient energy conversion in various industries.</p></p>
