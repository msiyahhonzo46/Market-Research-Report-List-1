<p><ul><li>Counter Electrode</li><li>Single Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The platinum electrode market comprises two main types: counter electrodes and single electrodes. Counter electrodes are used in electrochemical cells, serving as a reference point for measurements or as a return path for current, enhancing the efficiency of electrochemical reactions. Single electrodes, on the other hand, function independently to facilitate specific reactions, often in analytical applications. Both types leverage platinum's high conductivity and resistance to corrosion, making them essential components in various electrochemical and analytical processes.</p></p>
