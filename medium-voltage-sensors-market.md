<p><p>The Medium Voltage Sensors market includes various types designed for monitoring electrical parameters. Voltage sensors measure the electrical potential in medium voltage systems, while current sensors track the flow of electric current. PLC couplers facilitate communication between sensors and programmable logic controllers, enhancing automation and data collection. Combined sensors integrate both voltage and current measurement functionalities into a single device, optimizing space and improving efficiency. Together, these technologies play a critical role in ensuring the reliability and efficiency of medium voltage networks.</p></p>
