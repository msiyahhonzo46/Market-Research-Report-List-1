<p><p>Guided Wave Radar (GWR) liquid level sensors use electromagnetic waves to determine liquid levels in various applications. The market includes two primary types: probe-type and cable-type sensors. Probe-type sensors feature a single rod or probe that extends into the tank, making them suitable for smaller vessels. Cable-type sensors consist of a flexible cable that can be deployed in larger tanks, providing greater measurement versatility. Both types offer high accuracy and reliability, making them popular for industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and water treatment.</p></p>
