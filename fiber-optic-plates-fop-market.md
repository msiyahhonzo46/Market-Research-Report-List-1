<p><p>Fiber optic plates (FOP) are devices that manipulate light through fiber optic technology, widely used in imaging systems. The market consists of two main types: straight and tapered fiber optic plates. Straight FOPs maintain uniform size and shape, offering high-resolution imaging for applications like medical endoscopy and scientific instruments. Tapered FOPs, on the other hand, gradually change shape, enabling enhanced brightness and reduced size, making them ideal for compact systems and applications requiring high light transmission efficiency.</p></p>
