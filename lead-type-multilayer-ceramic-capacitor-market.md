<p><p>The Lead Type Multilayer Ceramic Capacitor market is categorized into radial and axial leaded configurations. Radial leaded multilayer ceramic capacitors feature leads extending in the same direction from the capacitor body, allowing for space-efficient mounting on circuit boards. In contrast, axial leaded multilayer ceramic capacitors have leads extending from either end of the cylindrical body, facilitating versatility in applications and easier installation in tight spaces. Both types offer reliable capacitance and reduced inductance, catering to various electronic applications.</p></p>
