<p><p>The Electronic Thermostatic Radiator Valves (eTRV) market comprises two main components: the valve head and the valve body. The valve head is the electronic component that regulates the temperature by adjusting the flow of hot water based on preset settings or room temperature readings. The valve body, on the other hand, is the physical part that connects to the radiator and houses the mechanical workings that control water flow. Together, they enhance energy efficiency and user comfort in heating systems.</p></p>
