<p><p>The commercial chillers market encompasses several types: </p><p>1. **Centrifugal Chillers** use a rotating impeller to compress refrigerant, making them efficient for large spaces.  </p><p>2. **Scroll Chillers** utilize two spiral elements to compress refrigerant, offering reliable performance with lower noise levels.  </p><p>3. **Screw Chillers** employ rotating screws for compression, ideal for large-scale cooling needs due to their high efficiency.  </p><p>4. **Other Chillers** include technologies like absorption chillers and air-cooled chillers, catering to various cooling requirements in commercial settings.</p></p>
