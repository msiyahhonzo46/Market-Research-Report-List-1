<p><strong>The Car Audio Crossovers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car audio crossovers are essential components that manage frequency distribution to speakers. The market is divided into two main types: active and passive crossovers. Active crossovers process audio signals before amplification, allowing for precise control of frequency ranges and often requiring external power. In contrast, passive crossovers operate after amplification, using passive components like capacitors and inductors, making them simpler but less customizable. Each type serves different user needs, influencing system performance and setup complexity in vehicle audio systems.</p></p>
